Advocacy & Impact

Your Business, Your Voice, Your Forum

What KBF Advocates For

We represent the collective interests of Kouga region businesses in local government, municipal planning, and economic development decisions.

🏛️

Municipal Engagement

KBF maintains active relationships with Kouga Municipality, attending council meetings, IDP (Integrated Development Plan) forums, and economic development forums to represent member interests.

📋

Policy Influence

We engage with proposed bylaws, zoning regulations, and municipal policies that affect business operations — from trading hours to signage regulations to licensing requirements.

🛣️

Infrastructure & Growth

Roads, water, electricity, and telecommunications infrastructure are critical to business survival. We advocate for infrastructure investment that enables economic growth in our region.

💼

Economic Development

We work with local government and external partners to attract investment, support SMME development, and create an enabling environment for existing businesses to thrive.

⚖️

Fair Licensing

Business licensing should be transparent, predictable, and fair. We push back against unnecessary barriers and advocate for streamlined processes that don't compromise compliance.

🌊

Tourism & Coastal Economy

As a coastal region, tourism is vital. We advocate for sustainable tourism growth, beach management policies that balance access and commerce, and investment in tourism infrastructure.

Current Priorities 2026

Key issues we are actively working on for our members this year.

Kouga SMME Development Strategy

We are engaging with the municipality on their draft SMME Development Strategy, ensuring that small business support programmes are accessible, well-funded, and actually reach the businesses that need them.

Infrastructure Maintenance & Upgrades

Potholes, water outages, and unreliable electricity affect every business. We are raising these issues systematically with municipal management and tracking response times.

Trading Hours & By-Law Review

We are participating in the review of municipal trading by-laws to ensure they reflect modern business realities, particularly for tourism-facing businesses and hospitality operators.

Broadband & Connectivity

Reliable internet is now a utility. We are advocating for public Wi-Fi in business districts and working with telecoms to improve coverage across the region.

Events & festivals Permitting

Local events drive tourism and community spirit. We are working with the municipality to create a clearer, faster events permitting process so organisers can plan with confidence.

How We Work

📝

Collect Issues

Members share challenges and concerns. We aggregate recurring themes and verify the scope of issues across the business community.

🏛️

Engage Decision-Makers

We present these issues directly to municipal officials, councillors, and other authorities through formal meetings, written submissions, and public forums.

📣

Keep Members Informed

Every member receives regular updates on advocacy progress — what's been raised, what's changed, and what to expect. No black boxes.

✅

Track Outcomes

We follow up. Commitments made by authorities are tracked and reported back. We celebrate wins and are transparent about delays.

Have an Issue to Raise?

KBF members can raise advocacy issues at any time. If something is affecting your business and you believe other Kouga businesses face the same challenge, let us know.
