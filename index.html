About Us

KBF is a non-profit organisation that strives to be the voice of business in the Kouga Region. We are focussed on ensuring the development of initiatives at municipal level to anticipate and find solutions to issues that affect our members.

Our mission is to exploit business opportunities in services, property, tourism, and agriculture to the benefit of our members.
