Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining KBF

Who can join the Kouga Business Forum?

Membership is open to any registered business, entrepreneur, or professional operating within the Kouga region, including Jeffreys Bay, Humansdorp, St. Francis Bay, and surrounding areas.

How do I receive my "Verified" badge?

Once your payment (Annual or Monthly) is processed and confirmed, our team will manually update your directory listing with the Verified Member badge within 48 hours.

What is the difference between Annual and Monthly membership?

Annual membership is a one-time payment of R1,200 for the calendar year, offering maximum value. Monthly membership requires a registration fee and R100 per month via a PayFast subscription.

How does KBF advocate for my business?

We maintain active relationships with the Kouga Municipality and other key stakeholders. We attend relevant council meetings and economic development forums to represent our members' collective interests.
