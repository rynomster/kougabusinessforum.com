About Kouga Business Forum

The voice of business in the Kouga Region. For over 15 years, we've been connecting entrepreneurs, leaders, and innovators across Jeffreys Bay, Humansdorp, St. Francis Bay, and beyond.

Our Mission Explore Directory

Our Mission

Empowering Kouga's business community through connection, advocacy, and continuous growth.

🎯

Connect & Grow

To create a thriving business ecosystem where local entrepreneurs can connect, collaborate, and grow their ventures through meaningful relationships and shared resources.

📢

Advocate & Amplify

To serve as the unified voice of Kouga businesses, advocating for policies and initiatives that benefit our community and amplify local business opportunities.

🤝

Empower & Support

To provide ongoing support, education, and resources that help businesses of all sizes navigate challenges and seize opportunities in the evolving economic landscape.

Our History

From humble beginnings to becoming the region's leading business network, our journey reflects Kouga's entrepreneurial spirit.

Founded in 2011

Kouga Business Forum was established by a group of local entrepreneurs who recognized the need for a stronger, more connected business community. The founders believed that collaboration would lead to greater opportunities for all.

First Annual Gathering

Our inaugural business summit brought together over 100 local business owners, setting the stage for regular networking events and collaborative initiatives that would define our early years.

Expansion & Recognition

As we grew, KBF expanded its reach across the Kouga region, establishing partnerships with local government, educational institutions, and business associations to amplify our impact.

Modern Era

Today, KBF continues to evolve, embracing digital tools and modern business practices while maintaining our core mission of connecting and empowering Kouga's business community.
