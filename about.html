About Kouga Business Forum

The voice of business in the Kouga Region. For over 15 years, we've been connecting entrepreneurs, leaders, and innovators across Jeffreys Bay, Humansdorp, St. Francis Bay, and beyond.

Our Mission Explore Directory

Our Vision

To be the voice of business in the Kouga Region.

Our Mission

To establish a credible and purposeful business forum that will through effective leadership and collective negotiation, further the interest of all members.

Our Story

Founded in 2011, Kouga Business Forum was established to create a platform where businesses across the Kouga region could connect, collaborate, and grow together.

What started as a small gathering of local entrepreneurs has evolved into the region's premier business network. Today, KBF serves hundreds of businesses across Jeffreys Bay, Humansdorp, St. Francis Bay, Hankey, Patensie, Loerie, and Thornhill.

Our commitment to collective growth and advocacy has made us the trusted voice for business in the Kouga Region, empowering our members through networking, education, and meaningful collaboration.

Proven Impact & Advocacy

Since 2011, KBF has been at the forefront of representing business interests and driving regional growth.

🏛️

Municipal Representation

We maintain active engagement with local government on critical issues including municipal rates assessments and bylaw implementations to ensure a fair business environment.

💧

Resource Management

The KBF Water Incentive Scheme is a prime example of our proactive approach to regional challenges, helping businesses implement sustainable water-saving practices.

🏗️

Economic Growth

We work to reduce bureaucratic friction for businesses, such as advocating for building plan amnesty to support local development and infrastructure investment.
